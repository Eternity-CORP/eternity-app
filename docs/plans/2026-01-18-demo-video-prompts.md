## Main Demo Video (45-60 seconds, 16:9)

```
Create a cinematic product demo video for E-Y crypto wallet app.

VISUAL STYLE:
- Dark, premium aesthetic with deep blacks (#000000)
- Glowing purple-to-cyan gradient accents
- Floating crystal shards (like infinity stones) as recurring motif
- Particle effects and subtle grid backgrounds
- Ultra-smooth 60fps motion
- iPhone 15 Pro mockup, floating in 3D space

SCENE 1 (0-8s) — PROBLEM
Camera slowly pushes through a chaotic digital void. Fragmented wallet addresses (0x7f3a...9b2c) float and glitch. Red warning symbols flash. Text appears: "Crypto is confusing." The chaos intensifies. Suddenly — darkness.

SCENE 2 (8-12s) — TRANSITION
A single glowing shard emerges from the darkness. It pulses with energy. More shards appear, orbiting slowly. They converge and explode into light. E-Y logo forms from particles.

SCENE 3 (12-25s) — BLIK DEMO
An iPhone floats into frame, rotating elegantly. The E-Y app is open.
- User taps "Send via BLIK"
- Enters amount: 50 USDC
- A beautiful 6-digit code appears: "847 291"
- Animated countdown timer: 2:00
- Text overlay: "6 digits. That's it."
- Second phone appears, enters the code
- Green checkmark animation, confetti particles
- Text: "Money sent. No addresses. No fear."

SCENE 4 (25-35s) — FEATURES MONTAGE
Quick cuts showcasing features, phone rotating between each:
- Token balances with USD values (coins animate in)
- @username registration (@alex appears with glow)
- Transaction history (items slide in smoothly)
- Split bill screen (avatars appear one by one)
Each transition has shard particles flying between cuts.

SCENE 5 (35-45s) — FUTURE VISION
Camera pulls back. The phone shrinks. Five glowing cards appear around it, orbiting like planets:
- "Network Abstraction"
- "SHARD Identity" (passport icon morphs into crystal)
- "Proof of Personhood"
- "AI Agent"
- "Fiat Ramp"
Cards pulse and glow. Text: "Coming Soon"

SCENE 6 (45-55s) — CLOSING
All elements converge into center. Massive shard forms.
E-Y logo appears, large and bold.
Tagline fades in: "The Wallet for Everyone"
Call to action: "Join the waitlist"
Shards orbit the logo as the video ends.

AUDIO DIRECTION:
- 0-8s: Tense, glitchy electronic sounds
- 8-12s: Bass drop, transition whoosh
- 12-45s: Uplifting electronic beat, builds momentum
- 45-55s: Music peaks, then resolves to calm ambient
- Throughout: Subtle UI sounds (taps, confirmations, whooshes)

MOOD:
Confident, futuristic, premium, trustworthy.
The feeling of "finally, crypto that makes sense."
```

## Usage Notes

1. **Main video** — For "Watch Demo" button on landing page
2. **Short version** — For social media promotion
3. **Hero loop** — Can be used as video background instead of Three.js (fallback or enhancement)

### Recommended Tools
- **Sora** (OpenAI) — Best quality, limited access
- **Runway Gen-3** — Good for product shots
- **Kling** — Good for longer videos
- **Pika** — Good for stylized content
- **HeyGen** — If need talking avatar

### Post-Processing
After AI generation, may need:
- Color grading to match brand colors
- Adding actual app screenshots (compositing)
- Sound design and music
- Text overlays with proper fonts
