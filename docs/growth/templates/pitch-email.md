## Email Best Practices

### Do:
- Keep subject lines short and specific
- Lead with the ask or most interesting point
- Include concrete numbers/metrics
- Make links easy to find
- Follow up (max 2 times)

### Don't:
- Write walls of text
- Ask for NDA before sharing deck
- Say "we have no competitors"
- Oversell or exaggerate metrics
- Spam or mass-email without personalization

### Timing:
- Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- Best time: 9-11 AM recipient's timezone
- Avoid: Monday morning, Friday afternoon
